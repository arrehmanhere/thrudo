How it works

Four steps from commitment to cashflow.

A clean, predictable on-ramp. Most investors are operational within four to six weeks of signing.

  1. 01 — Join

    Choose a package

    Select Starter, Growth or Premium. We sign, take a deposit and kick off onboarding.

  2. 02 — Build

    We build the store

    Brand, product research, suppliers, Shopify build and ad creative — done by our team in Lahore.

  3. 03 — Operate

    We run it daily

    Ads, customer service, fulfilment, supplier ops, optimisation. You receive monthly reports.

  4. 04 — Earn

    You take profit

    Profit is split each month after the management fee. You stay completely hands-free.
